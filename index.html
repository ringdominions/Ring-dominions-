Patch Notes

Progress
v1.6.3CurrentJun 22, 2026
  • Added free-look camera — drag anywhere on screen to orbit and tilt the view
  • Movement is now camera-relative with smoother, more agile turning
  • Added a Callsign/Username field so your name shows in the HUD
  • New "Player" fighter slot that displays your own callsign as its name
  • Added blood-splatter impact effects on melee and skill hits
  • Wider analog joystick response for finer movement control
v1.6.2StabilityJun 9, 2026
  • Added the Weapon Shop — buy and equip bats, hammers, blades & more
  • Added 10 new playable fighters to the hero roster
  • Rebuilt low-HP screen feedback with a sharper pulse effect
  • Fixed enemy health bars occasionally sticking after a kill
  • Minimap rendering optimized for smoother frame pacing
v1.5.0FeatureMay 14, 2026
  • Introduced quest tracking HUD with crystal & kill counters
  • Added XP bar and leveling feedback to the hero panel
  • New ambient lighting rig for the hero showcase screen
v1.4.0BalanceApr 2, 2026
  • Rebalanced speed/power/HP stats across the full roster
  • Tuned hostile aggro radius for fairer early encounters
  • Fixed joystick drift on certain touch devices
v1.2.0StabilityFeb 19, 2026
  • Mobile control layout overhaul for smaller screens
  • Fixed camera clipping through terrain on respawn
v1.0.0LaunchJan 8, 2026
  • First public build — open world adventure prototype
  • Core combat, movement and character select shipped
